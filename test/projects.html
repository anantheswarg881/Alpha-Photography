About Me!

Hello! This is Ananth. I'm a software professional passionate about photography. I have been clicking photos since three years now. I like clicking everything around especially, table top, landscapes, candid moments and fashion. I do this because I feel happy looking at the end result of my work and I like to look things in a more dramatic and beautiful way than they actually are. This is a life time learning for me and I'm happy to have recieved multiple awards/ publications for photography in the process. I hope to keep doing this as long as I can. If you need to get a good picture, let me know. I will try my best not to disappoint you.

I believe "Every thing you do is a reflection of what you are! Signature your work!"
Ananth Eswar
